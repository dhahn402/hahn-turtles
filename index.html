Why This Matters to Me

Costa Rica — the land of the turtles

As a young man at St. Augustine Prep in Richland, New Jersey, I went on a Third Semester trip to Costa Rica — the land of the turtles. Standing on those shores, watching the ocean the way God made it, is a memory I’ve carried my whole life.

“Some things you see once and they never leave you. The turtles are one of them.” — David Hahn

Sea turtles have crossed these oceans for more than a hundred million years. They were here long before us, and if we do our part, they’ll be here long after. This mission is my way of giving back to the water that gave me so much — and of teaching my family, and yours, to be good stewards of it.

Our Mission
Protect the waters. Steward the creatures. Enlarge the good.

We believe caring for creation is an act of worship. Through awareness, clean water, and real support for the people already protecting these turtles, we want to leave the ocean better than we found it — and inspire others to do the same, all over the world.
